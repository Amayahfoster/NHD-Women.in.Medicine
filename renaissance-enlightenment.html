Renaissance & Enlightenment Era (1500s-1700s)

The Renaissance and Enlightenment periods were crucial for women's advancement in medicine. Women like Elizabeth Blackwell, one of the first women to obtain a medical degree in the U.S., challenged gender norms to enter the medical profession and contribute to medical knowledge.
