Ancient & Medieval Era (Pre-1500s)

Women in the Ancient and Medieval eras were often excluded from formal medical education. Despite this, many women practiced herbal medicine, midwifery, and healing through spiritual means. These practices were often passed down through generations and were an integral part of communities.
